About Himalayan Jerseys

Our Story

Himalayan Jerseys was established in 2025 with a simple mission: to bring authentic cricket jerseys to fans across Nepal and beyond.

We understand the passion cricket fans have for their favorite teams and players. That's why we specialize in providing genuine jerseys from all cricket-playing nations and major leagues around the world.

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in cricket merchandise.
